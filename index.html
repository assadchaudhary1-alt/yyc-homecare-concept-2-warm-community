Home Care That May Already Be Covered

CDHCI Program

Alberta Health Services Funded Care

Alberta Health Services funds eligible clients through Alberta Blue Cross. No invoices. No out-of-pocket costs for approved hours. If your family qualifies, professional home care is covered — and we'll guide you through every step of the process.

VIP Program

Veterans Independence Program

The Veterans Independence Program supports Canadian veterans with in-home care. Your service deserves support in return. We're proud to be an approved VIP provider, helping veterans maintain their independence and quality of life at home.
